Game Rules

üéØ Objective:

Win tricks strategically to score points. First player to reach 5 points wins the game!

üÉè The Special Deck (30 Cards):

  • Regular Cards: 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King, Ace in all four suits (28 cards)
  • Special Cards: 7 of Hearts (‚ô•) and 7 of Spades (‚ô†) only (2 cards)
  • Card Power: 7 is the weakest, followed by 8, 9, 10, J, Q, K, and Ace is the strongest

üìã Setup Phase:

Each player receives 15 cards divided into three groups:

  • 5 Hand Cards: Cards you can see and play from (dealt first)
  • 5 Face-Up Cards: Visible cards on the table (dealt after trump selection)
  • 5 Face-Down Cards: Hidden cards beneath face-up cards (dealt after trump selection)

üé¥ Trump Selection - The Key Decision!

  • Players take turns selecting the trump suit each round
  • Look at your 5 hand cards and choose wisely!
  • The trump selector faces a tougher challenge:
    • Trump Selector's Target: 8 tricks
    • Opponent's Target: 7 tricks
  • Strategy Tip: Choose a suit where you have high cards (Kings, Aces) or many cards

üéÆ How to Play:

  • Leading: The starting player can play any card from their hand or face-up cards
  • Following: Must play the same suit if you have it (in hand or face-up cards)
  • Going Void: If you don't have the lead suit, you can play any card
  • Trump Power: Trump cards beat any non-trump card, regardless of rank!
  • Trick Winner:
    • Highest trump wins if any trump is played
    • Otherwise, highest card of the lead suit wins
  • Revealing Secrets: When you play a face-up card, the face-down card beneath it is revealed!

üèÜ Scoring System:

  • At the end of each round, count your tricks won
  • Points = How much you exceed your target
  • Examples:
    • Trump selector wins 10 tricks ‚Üí 10 - 8 = 2 points ‚úì
    • Trump selector wins 6 tricks ‚Üí 6 - 8 = negative, so 0 points
    • Opponent wins 9 tricks ‚Üí 9 - 7 = 2 points ‚úì
    • Opponent wins 5 tricks ‚Üí 5 - 7 = negative, so 0 points
  • Higher score is better! Try to exceed your target to score points

üéä Winning the Game:

  • The game continues through multiple rounds
  • First player to reach 5 points WINS!
  • Play aggressively - try to win more tricks than your target to score points!

üí° Pro Tips:

  • Pay attention to which cards have been played
  • Remember what face-up cards your opponent has
  • Sometimes winning fewer tricks is better!
  • Use trump cards wisely - they're powerful but limited
  • Watch when face-down cards get revealed
