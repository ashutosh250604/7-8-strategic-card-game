Game Rules

Game Concept:

A strategic 30-card trick-taking game for two players using a custom deck.

Deck Setup:

  • 30 cards: All ranks 8 through Ace (24 cards) + 7♥ and 7♠
  • 7s are lowest in their suits (7 < 8 < ... < A)

Card Distribution:

  • Each player gets 5 cards in hand
  • 5 face-down cards (hidden)
  • 5 face-up cards (covering face-down cards)

Trump Selection:

  • Alternates between players each round
  • Declarer chooses any suit as trump

Gameplay:

  • Players can play from hand or face-up piles
  • Playing face-up cards reveals the face-down card beneath
  • Must follow lead suit if possible
  • Can only play trump when void in lead suit or trump was led

Scoring:

  • Target is 7 tricks per round
  • Score = |tricks won - 7|
  • Game ends when either player reaches 10+ points
